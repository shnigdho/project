Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I provide when requesting a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, please include details about the type of cards needed, quantity, security features required, customization preferences, and project timeline.

What is the typical turnaround time for orders?

Turnaround times vary based on card type, quantity, and customization requirements. Standard orders typically take 2-4 weeks, while rush orders can be accommodated within 1-2 weeks when possible.

Do you offer samples before full production?

Yes, we provide sample cards for approval before proceeding with full production runs. Sample fees may apply but are typically credited toward your final order.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. For most smart cards, our minimum is 500 units, but we can discuss your specific needs for smaller runs.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to customers worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times will be included in your quotation.
